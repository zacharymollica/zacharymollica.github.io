My name is Zachary Mollica. I am a Canadian architect and maker interested in the integration of new digital tools alongside traditional analog ones. Right now I am a studio tutor for the Architectural Association’s Design + Make programme in Hooke Park, UK.

Growing up I worked with my father and grandfather - both carpenters. Before architecture school I studied liberal arts and physics. Since, I have worked for architecture and design practices in Amsterdam, Lunenburg, Toronto and Vancouver.

Variety of experience is my strongest trait. I like to work on a bit of everything.
