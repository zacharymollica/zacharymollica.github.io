Operable Bridge

Design + Make Induction Studio project. Along with a number of small physical tests, Grasshopper and Rhino were used to reimagine a traditional lattice truss bridge as an operable unit and to generate the fabrication information for its various components. The standard scissor unit becomes flexible with the addition of slotted scissor joints. Complete documentation of the investigation into scissor structures is available in the project portfolio.
