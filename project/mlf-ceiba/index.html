MLF: Ceiba

2020

Consulting work for experiential art collective Marshmallow Laser Feast.

Work with MLF provided me the opportunity to become incredibly familiar with a giant Ceiba Pentandra tree in the Amazon without being near it. Handed many gigabytes of data (photographs and LiDAR scans captured on site), I was tasked with taking on processing and alignment of both LiDAR and photogrammetry together. This raw data was carefully recoloured to remove variances, and handed over to the MLF studio team who produced absolutely magical illustrations for a larger exhibition that has since been included in shows in Quebec City, Stockhold, Seoul and London.
