SummerBuild 2016-

In 2016 I took over the running of the SummerBuild AA Visiting School with Jack Draper. SummerBuild is a yearly 4-week programme in which participants assist in the physical production of experimental architecture at the AA’s Hooke Park campus. Working on active projects participants are exposed to many aspects of timber building – from forestry operations, to traditional building methods, to innovative robotic fabrication.
