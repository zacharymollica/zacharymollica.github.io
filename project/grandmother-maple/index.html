Grandmother Maple

2021

3D scanning of a well known tree at Asitu’lisk which is believed to be hundreds of years old.

Sixty photos captured on a walk in the forest were processed through photogrammetry to create a rich three dimensional model of this special tree. Still and moving animations produced from this model present new ways to engage with and observe it. Images created for this project were recently published by The Philanthropist Journal in an article, ‘Choosing hope: Acts of reconciliation’.
