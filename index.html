An architect, educator, maker and maintainer who works with trees, my work integrates digital methods alongside craft and material knowledge in pursuit of better ways to build things at many scales.

Known for composing unusual bits of tree (Tree Fork Truss), I'm interested in applying tools of close observation – particularly 3D scanning – to material.

Whether found in the forest or city, I use tools close to hand to work on specific materials with inherent characteristics.
