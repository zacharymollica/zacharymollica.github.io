— About Zac

An architect, educator and maker who works with trees, my work integrates digital methods alongside craft and material knowledge in pursuit of better ways to build.

Known for composing unusual bits of tree (Tree Fork Truss), I'm interested in applying tools of close observation – particularly 3D scanning – to material.

Whether found in the forest or city, I work with specific materials rather than specifying general ones.
