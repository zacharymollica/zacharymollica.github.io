Hi,

My name is Zachary Mollica and I am a Canadian architect and maker. Completing the Architectural Association’s Design + Make programme in Hooke Park over the past two years, my work has become increasingly focussed on the integration of innovative digital methods alongside traditional craft knowledge.

Growing up I worked with my father and grandfather, both carpenters. I studied liberal arts and physics at university before entering Dalhousie School of Architecture. After completing my undergraduate studies I worked for architecture and design practices in Amsterdam, Lunenburg, Toronto and Vancouver.

Variety of experience is my strongest trait, I like to work on a bit of everything.
