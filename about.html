Architect and Maker

Hi,

My name is Zachary Mollica and I am an architect and maker currently based in Hooke Park, UK. Variety of experience is my strongest trait - I like to work on a bit of everything. Growing up I learned from my father and grandfather, both carpenters. At school I studied liberal arts and physics before entering architecture school. Later, I have worked for a number of architecture and design practices in Amsterdam (NL), Vancouver (BC), Toronto (ON) and Lunenburg (NS).

Over the past 16 months, I completed the the MArch Design & Make at the Architectural Association. The focus of my time in Hooke has been the realization of the Wood Chip Barn - a robotically fabricated structure composed of tree forks which spans 25m and a number of smaller projects. Through these projects I have become focussed on work involving the integration of digital and analogue techniques.

I am currently available for hire.
