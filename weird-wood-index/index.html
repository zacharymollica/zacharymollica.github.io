The Weird Wood Index

NOTE: This list is a work in progress that will in time become more whole. I have endeavoured to provide full lists of authors and affiliations where I have them, but assume many have been missed. If you have contributed and aren't noted, send me an email. Similarly, if you have an image of one of the projects to share that I don't have listed, please email these too. Critically, there are countless vernacular and other uses of 'weird wood' that have not been included in this list. At this time I am most interested in newer applications of these tree pieces starting around the 1980s.
