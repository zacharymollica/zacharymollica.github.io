Measured Architecture

2011

Internship with Measured Architecture in Vancouver (BC). Primarily involved in model making and schematic design, I worked closely with partner Clinton Cuddington throughout my time at Measured. As well as models, I was able to use my carpentry experience in the construction of full scale material samples and mockups to be used for material selection.
