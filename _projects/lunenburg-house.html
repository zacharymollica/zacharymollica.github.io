--- layout: project year: 2011 order: 12 comment: "House designed for Lunenburg, Nova Scotia in a studio led by architect Brian MacKay Lyons. Old Town Lunenburg is a UNESCO World Heritage Site due to the continued legibility of British grid iron plan used to establish a number of North American settlements. Responding to this, the house presents a modestly scaled front to its residential neighbours while growing towards the larger harbour buildings on the south side of its site." ---
