{{ page.title }}

2011

House designed for Lunenburg (NS) in a Dalhousie studio led by architect Brian MacKay Lyons. Old Town Lunenburg is a UNESCO World Heritage Site due to the continued clarity of British grid iron plan with which it was settled. The house presents a modest presence to its residential neighbours while growing towards the large harbour buildings on the south side of its site.
