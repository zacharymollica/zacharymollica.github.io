Initiated in 2016 with Director Emmanuel Vercruysse, Robotic Fabrications is a Visiting School based at the Architectural Association's Hooke Park campus. [2016] 'Chainsaw Choreographies' explored the precision, petrol-fuelled territory where code meets chainsaw. [2017] 'Bandsaw Manouevres' investigated the flexible limits of a tensed blade.
