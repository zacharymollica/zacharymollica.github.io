--- layout: project year: 2015 order: 1 comment: "Design + Make studio project. A robotically fabricated structure spanning 25m x 10m x 8m made from 20 disinct tree forks. The project focussed on developing a workflow for using complex pieces of timber commonly disregarded by the timber building industry. A survey of Hooke identified 204 potential forks in standing trees. 25 trees were felled with our forester. Detailed 3D scans were generated for each fork, and a script developed to optimise their arrangement. After machining each with a robotic arm, the forks were assembled in to two halves before moving to site." people: Martin Self, Emmanuel Vercruysse, Swetha Vegesana, Sahil Shah, Pradeep Devadass, Charlie Corry Wright, Jack Draper, Christopher Sadd, Tim Halbery ---
