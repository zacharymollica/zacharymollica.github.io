{{ page.title }}

2014

AA Design & Make Induction Studio project. Along with a number of small physical tests, Grasshopper and Rhino were used to reimagine a traditional lattice truss bridge as an operable unit and to generate the fabrication information for its various components. The unit becomes flexible with the addition of slotted scissor joints.
