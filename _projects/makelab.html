Tutor for Makelab 2016, a one week AA Visiting School in Hooke Park. This year's programme considered relationships between natural processes of formation and rule-based processes for architectural design. The result is a system based on a nesting of truncated octahedron whose apertures, arrangement and scale reflect data inputs from the site and material.

With: Jeroen van Ameijde, Alejandro Garcia Gadea, Yutao Song
